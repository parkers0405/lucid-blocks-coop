# BonkWithFriends

A seamless Drop-In, Drop-Out P2P multiplayer mod for Megabonk, powered by BepInEx and Steamworks.NET.

## Features
* **P2P Steam Networking:** Play seamlessly with your Steam friends without port-forwarding.
* **Full World Sync:** Synchronized interactables, chests, magnet shrines, and random world generation.
* **Combat Sync:** Synchronized weapons, projectiles, XP, level ups, and enemy physics.
* **Spectator Mode & Revives:** Watch your friends when you die, and let them revive you to keep the run going!
* **In-Game Chat:** Press `Enter` to open the overlay chat and talk to your lobby.

## Installation (Manual)
1. Install [BepInEx 6 IL2CPP](https://github.com/BepInEx/BepInEx/releases) into your Megabonk root folder.
2. Download the latest `BonkWithFriends.zip` from Thunderstore or Discord latest Releases.
3. Extract the contents (the `.dll` and any asset bundles) into your `BepInEx/plugins/` folder.
4. Launch the game!

## Multiplayer Guide
- **Host a Game:** In the main menu, click `Multiplayer` -> `Create Multiplayer Game`.
- **Join a Game:** In the main menu, click `Multiplayer` -> Have the Host send you a Steam Invite or right-click them in your Steam Friends List and select `Join Game`.
